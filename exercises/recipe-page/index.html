DESSERT

CHOCOLATE ALMOND CROISSANT ROLLS

Think chocolate almond croissant, but ten times easier. Buttery, fluffy rolls filled with creamy almond goodness and melty chocolate, then topped with toasty almonds for that golden crunch. They taste like they came straight from a akery—except you made them in under an hour.

PREP TIME: 25 MINUTES COOK TIME: 15 MINUTES

TOTAL TIME: 40 MINUTES YIELDS: 12

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pack (12 rolls) King’s Hawaiian sweet rolls
  • 1/2 cup milk or semi-sweet chocolate chips or chopped
    • chocolate chunks
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  • powdered sugar, for dusting on top

ALMOND SIMPLE SYRUP

  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract

ALMOND FILLING

  • 3/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/3 cup + 1 tablespoon granulated white sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 large egg
